# Peptides for cognition

:::warning Educational use only
This page is informational and is **not medical advice**. It does not recommend any peptide
and does not provide dosing or protocols. Most cognition-related peptide claims rest on
preclinical or single-country research and anecdotal reports. Consult a qualified clinician
before making any decision.
:::

Cognition covers focus, memory, mental clarity, and mood. Many of the peptides discussed here
come predominantly from Russian research literature and are not approved in Western markets,
so the independent evidence base is limited. The links below show the strongest tier of
evidence that currently applies.

## Relevant peptides

- [Semax](../peptides/semax.md) (Preclinical, Mechanistic, Anecdotal): nootropic and neuroprotective research, mostly Russian-sourced.
- [Selank](../peptides/selank.md) (Preclinical, Mechanistic, Anecdotal): anxiolytic and mood research, mostly Russian-sourced.

See [how evidence tiers work](../how-it-works.md) to interpret these labels.
