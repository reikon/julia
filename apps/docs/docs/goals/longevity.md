# Peptides for longevity

:::warning Educational use only
This page is informational and is **not medical advice**. It does not recommend any peptide
and does not provide dosing or protocols. Longevity is a goal where marketing far outpaces
evidence; most claims here are preclinical or mechanistic. Consult a qualified clinician
before making any decision.
:::

Longevity and healthy aging is a popular but evidence-thin category. Human lifespan or
healthspan outcomes are essentially impossible to demonstrate with the short, small studies
that exist for most of these peptides, so claims should be read with particular caution. The
links below show the strongest applicable evidence tier.

## Relevant peptides

- [Epitalon](../peptides/epitalon.md) (Preclinical, Mechanistic): aging and pineal research needing independent confirmation. Not approved.
- [MOTS-c](../peptides/mots-c.md) (Preclinical, Mechanistic): mitochondrial-derived peptide studied in aging biology. Not approved.
- [GHK-Cu](../peptides/ghk-cu.md) (Clinical, Preclinical, Mechanistic): clinical for topical skin endpoints; broader claims preclinical.
- [Sermorelin](../peptides/sermorelin.md) (Clinical, Mechanistic): GH-axis history; longevity use is anecdotal.

See [how evidence tiers work](../how-it-works.md) to interpret these labels.
